for system owners

With feed in tariffs on the way out it is crucial now that system owners understand how to use their solar generation in the best possible way. Our user friendly dashboards and reports put the power back in your hands to better manage your power within the home or business.

Some of the benefits:

  • monitor the health of your solar system
  • monitoring the import and export of power for your home or business
  • compare your system performance with others in the community
  • improve your ROI through better use of the solar generation
  • check the electricity company is billing you correctly
