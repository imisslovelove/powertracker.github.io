Solutions for Solar Professionals

For solar professionals
We can provide a web based dealer portal to monitor and manage your entire customer base. The web based portal provides you with all the tools required to minimise your ongoing operations and to provide your clients with a complete energy solution.

Some of the benefits:

  • minimise your support calls
  • avoid unnecessary truck roll outs
  • generate another stream of revenue
  • improve your current value proposition with the end client
  • provide remote monitoring and diagnostics
For the system owners
With feed in tariffs on the way out it is crucial now that system owners understand how to use their solar generation in the best possible way. Our user friendly dashboards and reports put the power back in your hands to better manage your usage within the home or business.

Some of the benefits:

  • monitor the health of your solar system
  • monitoring the import and export of power for your home or business.
  • compare your system performance with others in the community
  • improve your ROI through better use of the solar generation
  • check the electricity company is billing you correctly
