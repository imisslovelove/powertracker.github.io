Our products help you enhance the performance of your buildings whilst providing the end client with a more sustainable lifestyle. Working closely with leaders in sustainable property development we have developed a solution to help evaluate the efficiency of current and future developments to enhance their performance in future buildings. Using state of art hardware and mobile technologies we can begin to provide building's baseline energy consumption well before handing them over. This baseline energy information can then be compared years later to provide an insight into the buildings efficiency not usually seen.

some of the benefits

  • helps prove your building are green and sustainable
  • provides valuable feedback to improve future builds e.g. average power consumption per m2
  • interactive foyer display screens to promote responsible CO2 and power management to the end client
  • seamless installation from construction to client handover through API integration and 3G technologies
  • sub metering capability of commons services and individual tenants
  • management portals for strata managements across multiple premises
  • integration with strata management existing customer portals to improve customer experience and satisfaction

We have developed a streamlined service for green builders, strata management and tenants that is simple and cost effective.
