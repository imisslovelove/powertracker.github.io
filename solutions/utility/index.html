Why do Utilities need Power Tracker?
With the ever increasing demand on utilities to retain their customers, it is now up to Utilities to value add services or be at risk of losing the customer.
Increase average revenue per subscriber
In addition to supplying electricity, utilities can expand the range of services offered to customers while improving their current business models and provide new sources of both near-term and ongoing recurring revenue.
Increase customers satisfaction and loyalty
Customer satisfaction is directly linked to customer loyalty. Providing customers with an opportunity to save money is a method to strengthen satisfaction and loyalty. Power Tracker gives customers a much needed insight into residential power and energy usage. They can monitor, track and analyze energy usage thus lower energy bills up to 20% or more.
Reduce customers churn rate
Power Tracker is an effective solution to fight customer churn. Utilities can now offer new value added products/services, create unique customer experiences and further drive market/competitive differentiation.
Leverages existing network infrastructure
Utilities can leverage existing network infrastructure while further extending reach inside the home. Power Tracker creates new business models with any infrastructure upgrades or network capital expenditures (CAPEX).
Bring GREEN and carry out Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)
Corporations are implementing and embracing more sustainable (Green) approaches to business. This CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) now extends beyond the enterprise out to the customers. Utilities should educate and facilitate end user participation with real-time access to energy usage, monitoring, control and conservation.
