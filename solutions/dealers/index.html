Becoming a Dealer is easy and it's free.
Simply sell our products to your client base and we will provide you with your very own Dealer portal for free. As a Dealer your customers will log into your ?branded? web interface and you will also have the ability to manage all your customers through your manager account access.
The Dealer portal will provide you with all the tools and reporting needs to provide your customers with the very support and service.
Some of the key benefits for Dealers:
  • inventory management of all your clients equipment
  • a great energy evaluation tool to help you sell other energy efficient products
  • reporting across multiple sites to detect trends and patterns
  • promoting your own brand
  • additional revenue stream
  • supplements existing product and service offerings
  • provide energy assessments
  • help estimate energy saving potentials
  • identify customers energy concerns and needs
  • 3G capabilities to provide a solution to the customer running completely your own infrastructure
  • test new energy efficient technologies
  • in depth data analysis right down to the minute with readings of Active Power (W), Total Accumulated Electricity Consumption (kWh), Interval Electricity Consumption
